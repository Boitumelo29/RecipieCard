Spaghetti Carbonara

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste

1. Cook spaghetti in boiling water according to package instructions.
2. In a separate pan, cook pancetta until crispy. Remove excess fat.
3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
4. Drain spaghetti and add to the pan with pancetta. Mix well.
5. Remove pan from heat and quickly stir in egg mixture until well combined.
6. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan on top.
